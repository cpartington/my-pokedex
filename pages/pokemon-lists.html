Chuffin’s Pokémon Lists: Sword & Shield

This page is still a work in progress, so check back later for updates!

Current Team

Most of my pokémon knowledge has been provided by my brother. This is my current lineup:

  1. Cinderace. My starter pokémon with improved stats via breeding. I guess he's cute enough.
  2. Rhyperior. He’s pretty useful, but not very cute.
  3. Dracovish. Pretty ugly pokémon but he does the job. I used to use Gastrodon which is way cuter.
  4. Gourgeist. Of course my brother makes fun of this pokémon but she’s quite cute. And pretty useful, especially against Drednaw.
  5. Zacian. I have the sword edition so this is my go-to for steel pokémon (although far from the cutest one). I don’t actually use it that often.

Favorite Pokémon

A list of my favorite pokémon, regardless of stats:

  • Eevee and all its evolutions. Easily my favorite pokémon of all time.
  • Raichu. He's pretty cute and less overrated than Pikachu.
  • Ponyta (Galarian). So heckin cute.
  • Liepard. Very pretty! Evolves from Purrloin which is also cute.
  • Yamper. Heckin doge, much cute. And its evolution, Boltund, is very useful.
  • Vulpix. So cute and pretty!
