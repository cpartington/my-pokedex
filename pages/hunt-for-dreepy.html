The less-long hunt for Dreepy

It was a foggy day and I was on the hunt for an elusive pokémon the Internet claimed to be good. Before this, I halfheartedly ran around in the grass for several days hoping to come across a Dreepy. The Internet said there was a 1% chance any given day, but today was a foggy day, which meant there was a 2% chance.

Today was the day. I started searching, running into several Corviknights. And then I saw it, a strange-looking pokémon with Dreepy on its head. Could this be the mysterious Drakloak I’d heard about?

When I got closer, there were three of them. You can bet I caught all of them. (There was a fourth one that ended up being a casualty.) I couldn’t believe my luck. I had one male and two females.

I really wanted to evolve one of them into Dragapult, but I couldn’t tell which one was the best. I’d heard of EVs and IVs before, but I had no idea what they were. Thus began my search to find out how good my pokémon were.
